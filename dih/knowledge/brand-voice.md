## Who we are
Dog is Human makes [PRODUCT CATEGORY] for dogs, built on [KEY DIFFERENTIATOR].

## Voice
- Warm, plain-spoken, a little witty. Talks to dog owners like a knowledgeable friend.
- Confident but never hypey. No miracle claims.
- Short sentences. Lead with the dog owner's problem.

## Always
- Center the dog's comfort and the owner's peace of mind.
- Use concrete, sensory language ("stops the 3am scratching").

## Never (compliance)
- No medical/curative claims ("cures", "treats", "heals").
- No competitor names.
- No guarantees of results.

## Hook patterns that work
- Call out the symptom ("If your dog won't stop scratching...")
- Contrast old vs. new ("You've tried everything. Here's what actually helped.")
- Curiosity ("The reason your dog's ears keep flaring up isn't what you think.")

## CTA
- Soft, single action: "Tap to see if it's right for your dog."

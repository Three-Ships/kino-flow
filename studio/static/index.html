        🎬 Variants — VO + B-roll
        
      
                

      
                
        
                
          Kino writes the script N times (hook + body + CTA, different angles each, respecting brand guidelines under the team), generates a VO for each via ElevenLabs, composites with a random b-roll sequence from your folder, and burns captions. One click → N finished videos.
        
                

        
        
                
          💡 Leave the script blank to auto-generate from Brand Guidelines — Kino reads <asset_root>/<team>/Brand Guidelines/, picks the topic + angle itself, writes the VO, and lays b-roll under it. Requires a brand guideline doc in that folder.
        
                

        
                
          
          
        
                

        
                
          
        
                

        
                
          
          
          
        
                

        
                
          
          
        
                

        

        
                
          Captions are forced to center frame (Meta-safe); each variant's hook line is also burned as a top-of-frame overlay inside the IG/FB safe zone. Brand guidelines are auto-discovered at <asset_root>/<team>/Brand Guidelines/. Output lands in videos/edit/variants_<timestamp>/.
